PRICING

Every photoshoot is entirely unique, which is often reflected in my pricing. I offer a variety of pre-built packages but will gladly work with you to tailor your photoshoot to meet your specific needs. Generally, additional hours are less expensive than the first hour, and the third less expensive than the second and so on. If you have any questions or concerns about pricing, don't hesitate to reach out.

PAYMENT

I require a 50% non-refundable deposit in order for you to book your shoot. Once I receive the deposit, your date is saved and your shoot is locked in. If you would like to set up a payment plan, with any number of payments over time I can do that, just know that you will not receive your photos until payment is made in full. If you have any questions at all, feel free to reach out to me directly. I take cash, Venmo, all major credit cards, or ACH transfers.
