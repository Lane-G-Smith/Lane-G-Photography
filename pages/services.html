PRICING

Some photographers won't tell you their prices until you contact them, which can lead to you feeling pressured and forced into a sales negotiation. I don't believe in that. That being said, every photoshoot is entirely unique, which is often reflected in my pricing, and I will gladly work with you to tailor your photoshoot and price-point to meet your specific needs. Generally, this is more or less what you can expect when choosing me as your photographer. I do not charge more or less based on the type of photoshoot that you need.

One Hour | $395 | 50+ photos
Two Hours | $495 | 75+ photos
Three Hours | $595 | 100+ photos
Travel | $2/mile from Sedona

PAYMENT

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required in order to book your photoshoot. Once the deposit is received, your date is saved and your photoshoot is locked in. The other 50% of payment is due on the day of your photoshoot. If you have any questions at all, feel free to reach out to me directly. I take cash, Venmo, all major credit cards, or ACH transfers.
